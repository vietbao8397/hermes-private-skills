# Personal Brand Monetize (Global) — Funnels, Offers, Tax/Legal

> Build authority -> earn trust -> close revenue. This skill focuses on step 3 (close revenue) after you have context (skill 22), strategy, and content. Three funnels for founder / coach / creator archetypes plus region-specific tax and legal guidance.

---

## For newbies

### When to start monetizing

DO NOT monetize too early. Wait for these 3 conditions before launching your first offer:

1. **Audience of 1,000+ quality followers** — engagement above 3%, thoughtful comments, DMs asking specific questions
2. **Locked-in pillar and niche** — you know exactly "who you help with what" (see skill 22). Example: "Pricing coach for 1-on-1 coaches scaling to premium prices" — NOT vague "personal branding coach"
3. **Real proof** — past projects with ROI for clients, managed a meaningful budget, shipped 50+ long-form pieces

### 5 common monetization mistakes

1. **Launching an offer before having an audience** — pre-launch course with zero followers -> zero sales
2. **Pricing too low** — $19 course in 4 modules -> audience reads it as "cheap" -> trust evaporates
3. **No offer ladder** — only one $2,000 product, audience not ready to pay -> lost lead
4. **Constant discounting** — pre-order 50% off -> late buyers wait for sales -> audience trained to wait
5. **No focus** — running coaching + course + affiliate + brand deals at once -> nothing reaches scale

### Realistic time investment

- **Build audience phase 1:** 6-12 months (0 -> 1,000 quality followers)
- **Soft launch first offer:** 2-3 months after audience stabilizes
- **Stable monetization:** 12-18 months after starting personal brand
- This is NOT "make money fast" — it's a long-term asset, with the highest ROI hitting in years 2-3

### 3 monetization archetypes

| Archetype | Audience size to monetize | Primary income | Time to first $1,000 |
|-----------|--------------------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Founder | 500-2,000 (quality) | 1-on-1 advisory, speaking | 6-9 months |
| Coach | 1,000-5,000 | Course, cohort, 1-on-1 | 4-8 months |
| Creator | 5,000-20,000+ | Brand deal, affiliate, course | 8-12 months |

> Founders can monetize with smaller audiences because deal sizes are large (1 advisory engagement = $5,000-30,000). Creators need larger audiences because deal sizes are smaller (1 brand deal = $500-5,000 at 5K-20K followers).

## Information collection (4 questions max)

Ask up to 4 questions before recommending a funnel:

1. **Current audience size?** Total followers + email list + primary platform (LinkedIn / FB / TikTok / IG / X / Newsletter)
2. **Specific niche?** The topic you own (example: "marketing for SME f&b in [region]" — NOT vague "marketing")
3. **Current revenue from personal brand?** Are you selling anything yet? Price? Volume? (so we know the starting line)
4. **12-month target?** Monthly income target + hours/week willing to invest

## Funnel per archetype

### Founder Funnel

```
LinkedIn long-form 2-3 posts/week
    -> Newsletter signup CTA
    -> Weekly insights email
    -> Lead score (high-intent vs mid-intent)
        High intent -> 1-on-1 discovery call (30 min free)
        Mid intent -> Workshop ($400)
    -> Advisory ($5,000 / 3 months)
    -> Speaking gig referral
```

**Benchmark conversions:** LinkedIn -> Newsletter 5-12%; Newsletter -> Discovery call 1-3%; Discovery call -> Advisory 30-50%.

### Coach Funnel

```
IG / FB / TikTok daily content
    -> Free guide PDF (lead magnet)
    -> 7-day email nurture sequence
    -> Self-segment by level
        Beginner -> Mini-course ($147)
        Mid level -> Cohort ($1,200)
        Advanced -> 1-on-1 coaching ($4,000)
    -> Retreat / Mastermind referral
```

**Benchmark conversions:** Content -> Lead magnet 3-8%; Lead -> Mini-course 2-5%; Mini-course -> Cohort 15-25%; Cohort -> 1-on-1 10-20%.

### Creator Funnel

```
TikTok / IG / YouTube daily content
    -> Affiliate link bio (passive income)
    -> Brand deal inbound pitch
    -> Email list signup (long-term asset)
    -> Own product launch
    -> Membership / Patreon
```

**Benchmark conversions:** View -> Affiliate click 0.5-2%; Click -> Sale 2-8%; View -> Email signup 0.3-1%; Email -> Course buyer 2-6%.

## Pricing Psychology

### Authority pricing (premium)

Once you have proof (case studies, testimonials, media mentions), price by **authority**, NOT by hours.

- 1-on-1 coaching 3 months IS NOT 12 sessions x $100/session = $1,200
- 1-on-1 coaching 3 months IS "Transform your business / life" = **$4,000-8,000**

Formula: Outcome value / Time saved / Risk removed. Clients do NOT buy hours; they buy **results**.

### Value-based pricing (B2B advisory)

For founder advisory:

```
Price = 5-10% of the ROI the client can realistically capture in 12 months
```

Example: Client with $500K annual revenue, advisory targets 30% lift ($150K). Fair price = $7,500-15,000/quarter = **$2,500-5,000/month**.

### Anchor pricing (course launch)

When launching a course, set 3 tiers:
- Tier A (high anchor): $997 — full mentorship + cohort + 1-on-1
- Tier B (target): $497 — full course + community
- Tier C (entry): $197 — full course self-paced

**70% of buyers pick Tier B** because Tier A anchors. Without Tier A, Tier B feels expensive.

### Currency choice (Global audience)

| Audience | Currency | Reasoning |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| US / Global B2B | USD | Standard for global digital business |
| EU primary | EUR (with USD secondary) | Local trust + global option |
| UK | GBP | Local trust |
| LATAM B2B | USD | Inflation-protected, professional |
| LATAM B2C | Local currency (BRL, MXN) | Familiarity, installments |
| SEA mixed | USD + local | Pte Ltd SG often invoices USD |

### 3 anti-patterns

1. **Hourly rate cap** — coach $50/hour -> client buys 4 hours for $200 -> audience reads "cheap coach" -> no perceived value
2. **Fixed price forever** — never raise prices -> new audience sees same price as old audience -> no urgency to buy now
3. **Discounts >30%** — frequent 50% off sales -> audience trained to wait for sales, never buys full price

## Outreach: Inbound vs Outbound

### Inbound (warm — content-driven)

- **SEO long-form:** 2,000-word articles ranking on Google -> 3-12 months ROI
- **Social content:** LinkedIn / FB / IG / TikTok -> 1-3 months to first leads
- **Referrals:** Past clients refer new ones -> free, conversion above 50%
- **Newsletter:** Warm list, 5-15% conversion for mid-tier offers

**Conversion benchmark:** Inbound lead -> Buyer **3-15%** (high because pre-qualified).

### Outbound (cold — proactive)

- **Cold DM LinkedIn:** 50-100 DMs/week -> 5-15% reply -> 1-2% book call
- **Cold email:** 100-200 emails/week -> 3-8% reply -> 0.5-1% book call
- **Paid ads:** Meta / TikTok / Google ads -> 1-3% landing conversion -> 10-20% nurture -> buyer

**Conversion benchmark:** Outbound lead -> Buyer **0.5-3%** (low because cold).

### When to use which

| Situation | Inbound | Outbound |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Audience above 2K | Yes (primary) | Optional |
| Audience below 500, need leads fast | No | Yes |
| Deal size above $5K (advisory) | Yes (main) | Yes (supplement) |
| Deal size below $200 (course) | Yes (main) | Paid ads |
| Niche B2B (small TAM) | Yes | Cold email works |
| Niche B2C (mass) | Yes | Paid ads main |

> **Best combo:** Inbound content for 12 months (build trust) + Outbound cold DM for large deals (advisory) + Paid ads for course launches (booster).

## Brand Deal Negotiation for Creators

### Floor price formula

```
Floor price (USD) = CPM_USD x Follower / 1000 x Engagement multiplier x Format multiplier
```

- **CPM (global, varies by niche):** $5-15 (TikTok), $8-20 (IG), $4-12 (FB), $15-50 (LinkedIn B2B), $30-100 (YouTube long-form)
- **Engagement multiplier:** 0.5x (ER below 2%), 1x (ER 2-5%), 1.5x (ER 5-10%), 2x (ER above 10%)
- **Format multiplier:** 1x (story), 2x (post feed), 3x (reel / short video), 4x (livestream / long-form video)

**Example:** Creator with 50K TikTok, ER 6%, brand wants 1 video. Floor = $10 x 50 x 1.5 x 3 = **$2,250**. Brand offers $750 -> DO NOT accept, counter at $1,800.

### Negotiable contract terms

| Term | Creator-friendly | Brand-friendly |
|------|------------------|----------------|
| Exclusivity | 7-30 days max (NOT perpetual) | Brand wants 90+ days |
| Usage rights | 30-90 days organic, +50% fee for paid ads | Brand wants perpetual + ads |
| Whitelisting (running ads on your handle) | +30-50% fee | Brand likes for authenticity |
| Revisions | Up to 2 rounds | Brand wants unlimited |
| Cancellation fee | 50% if cancelled within 7 days | Brand wants free cancellation |
| Payment terms | 50% upfront, 50% on post | Brand wants Net 30-60 |

### 5 negotiation tactics

1. **Don't quote first** — ask the brand's budget ("What's your budget for this collaboration?")
2. **Have a rate card** — send media kit + price list. Signals professionalism, not negotiable bargaining
3. **Bundle deals** — 1 reel + 3 stories = 1.3x the price of 1 reel (NOT 4x — fair to the brand)
4. **Raise rates 20-30% per quarter** after 2-3 deals — train brands to expect rate increases
5. **Decline below floor** — accepting below floor once -> agency network shares your low rate -> next brands offer the same

### Regional rate differential

- US creator with 50K followers = $1,500-3,000/post
- Singapore creator with 50K = SGD 800-1,500 (~$600-1,100)
- LATAM creator with 50K = $300-900
- SEA non-SG creator with 50K = $200-700

**Why the gap:** Brand budgets are smaller; agency middlemen take 20-30% cut; creator supply is higher. To raise rates: build niche authority (B2B / fintech / luxury) -> CPMs are 3-5x mass entertainment.

## Inbound Lead Tracking

### DM tracking spreadsheet (Google Sheets / Notion / Airtable)

| Column | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Date | 2026-05-08 |
| Source | LinkedIn DM / IG DM / Email / Referral |
| Name | Jane Doe |
| Company | XYZ Inc. |
| Niche fit | Yes / No |
| Stage | Cold / Warm / Hot / Booked / Closed / Lost |
| Offer interest | Workshop / Advisory / Course / Coaching |
| Estimated deal value | $5,000 |
| Next action | Discovery call 2026-05-12 |
| Notes | Pain point: pricing strategy |

> **Goal:** One glance, know the next step. Update weekly minimum.

### Conversion benchmark (warm inbound DM)

- DM -> Reply: 70-90% (warm audience)
- Reply -> Discovery call: 30-50%
- Discovery call -> Proposal: 50-70%
- Proposal -> Closed: 30-50%

**Total: DM -> Closed ~ 5-15% (warm inbound).**
