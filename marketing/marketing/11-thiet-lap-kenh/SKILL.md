Hoi user toi da 4 cau sau truoc khi bat dau:

1. **Ban muon thiet lap kenh nao?** (TikTok Brand / Zalo OA / Facebook Page / Email Brevo / TikTok Shop / Instagram / Google Business Profile — co the chon nhieu)
