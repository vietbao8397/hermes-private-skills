## Singapore (SG) — Most foreign-friendly

Singapore is a top destination for global creators / founders due to its low corporate tax, strong banking, and English-language legal system.

### Entity choices

| Entity | Setup cost | Tax treatment |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | SGD 65 (ACRA) | Personal income tax 0-24% (resident) |
| **Pte Ltd (Private Limited)** | SGD 315 (ACRA) | Corporate tax 17% (effective ~8.5% on first SGD 200K with rebates) |

### Pte Ltd tax benefits

- First SGD 100K of chargeable income: 75% tax exemption (effective 4.25%)
- Next SGD 100K: 50% tax exemption (effective 8.5%)
- Above SGD 200K: full 17%

**Effective tax on first SGD 200K:** ~SGD 12,750 (6.4%)

### GST (Goods and Services Tax)

- **Rate:** 9% (from 1 Jan 2024)
- **Registration threshold:** SGD 1M annual turnover
- Below threshold: voluntary registration available
- Digital services to overseas consumers: zero-rated (export)

### Personal income tax (resident)

| Income bracket SGD | Rate |
|--------------------|------|
| 0 - 20K | 0% |
| 20K - 30K | 2% |
| 30K - 40K | 3.5% |
| 40K - 80K | 7% |
| 80K - 120K | 11.5% |
| 120K - 160K | 15% |
| 160K - 200K | 18% |
| 200K - 240K | 19% |
| 240K - 280K | 19.5% |
| 280K - 320K | 20% |
| 320K - 500K | 22% |
| 500K - 1M | 23% |
| Above 1M | 24% |

### Foreigner / EP / S-Pass

- Tax resident: in SG 183+ days
- Non-resident: flat 22-24% on Singapore-source income only
- Many global creators incorporate Pte Ltd while residing elsewhere (with proper tax planning)

### CPF (mandatory savings) — for residents only

- Employer 17% + Employee 20% on wages up to SGD 6,000/month
- Self-employed: contribute to MediSave only

## Malaysia (MY)

### Entity choices

| Entity | Setup cost | Tax treatment |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Sole Proprietorship (Enterprise)** | MYR 30-60 (SSM) | Personal income tax 0-30% |
| **Sdn Bhd (Private Limited)** | MYR 1,000-2,000 | Corporate tax 17% (under MYR 600K chargeable) / 24% standard |

### Sdn Bhd benefits for SMEs

- Paid-up capital below MYR 2.5M + revenue below MYR 50M = SME tax rate
- First MYR 150,000 chargeable income: 15% (Budget 2024)
- MYR 150K - 600K: 17%
- Above MYR 600K: 24%

### SST (Sales and Service Tax)

- **Sales tax:** 5% or 10% on manufactured goods
- **Service tax:** 6% on taxable services (including digital services)
- **Registration threshold:** MYR 500K (services), MYR 500K (sales)

### Personal income tax

Progressive 10 brackets from 0% (MYR 0-5K) to 30% (above MYR 2M). Common middle brackets: 11% (MYR 50K-70K), 19% (MYR 70K-100K), 25% (MYR 100K-400K).

## Indonesia (ID)

### Entity choices

| Entity | Setup cost | Tax treatment |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Perseorangan / UD** | IDR 500K-2M | Personal PPh + Final Tax 0.5% (UMKM) |
| **PT Perseorangan** (single-owner PT, post-2021) | IDR 1-5M | Corporate tax 11-22% |
| **PT (Perseroan Terbatas)** | IDR 10-20M | Corporate tax 22%; 11% for first IDR 4.8B revenue (SME) |

### Final tax for SMEs (UMKM)

- Revenue below IDR 4.8B/year: 0.5% final tax on revenue (PP 23/2018)
- Simple, but cannot claim deductions

### VAT (PPN — Pajak Pertambahan Nilai)

- **Rate:** 11% (12% from 2025)
- **Registration threshold:** IDR 4.8B annual turnover
- Digital service tax (PMSE) on foreign digital providers selling to ID consumers

### Personal income tax

| Income IDR | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| Up to 60M | 5% |
| 60M - 250M | 15% |
| 250M - 500M | 25% |
| 500M - 5B | 30% |
| Above 5B | 35% |

## Thailand (TH)

### Entity choices

| Entity | Setup cost | Tax treatment |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Sole Proprietor** | THB 1,000-3,000 | Personal income tax 0-35% |
| **Limited Company** | THB 7,000-15,000 | Corporate tax 20% (SME 0-20% sliding) |

### SME tax rates

- First THB 300K: 0%
- THB 300K - 3M: 15%
- Above THB 3M: 20%

(Eligibility: paid-up capital below THB 5M + revenue below THB 30M)

### VAT

- **Rate:** 7% (reduced from standard 10% — extended yearly)
- **Registration threshold:** THB 1.8M annual turnover
- Foreign digital service tax: e-Service Act since 2021 (foreign platforms register)

### Personal income tax

Progressive 8 brackets from 0% (THB 0-150K) to 35% (above THB 5M). Common middle brackets: 10% (THB 300K-500K), 20% (THB 750K-1M), 25% (THB 1M-2M).

### LTR Visa (Long-Term Resident)

10-year visa for Wealthy Global Citizens / Pensioners / Work-from-Thailand Professionals / Highly-Skilled Professionals. Flat 17% income tax for highly-skilled professionals; tax exemption on overseas-sourced income for the other 3 categories.

## Philippines (PH)

### Entity choices

| Entity | Setup cost | Tax treatment |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Single Proprietorship** | PHP 1,000-3,000 (DTI) | Personal income tax 0-35% |
| **Corporation** | PHP 15,000-50,000 | Corporate tax 25% (20% if income below PHP 5M AND assets below PHP 100M) |
| **One Person Corporation (OPC)** (post-2019) | PHP 15,000-30,000 | Same as Corp |

### Train Law personal income tax (CREATE Act updates)

| Income PHP | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| 0 - 250,000 | 0% |
| 250,001 - 400,000 | 15% (was 20%, reduced 2023) |
| 400,001 - 800,000 | 20% (was 25%) |
| 800,001 - 2,000,000 | 25% (was 30%) |
| 2,000,001 - 8,000,000 | 30% |
| Above 8,000,000 | 35% |

### 8% optional tax (for SMEs)

Self-employed below PHP 3M gross sales/year can elect:
- 8% tax on gross sales/receipts above PHP 250K
- In lieu of personal income tax + percentage tax
- Simple, attractive for solo creators

### VAT

- **Rate:** 12%
- **Registration threshold:** PHP 3M annual gross sales
- Below threshold: 3% percentage tax (Other Percentage Tax)

## Best country for global creators (SEA)

Personal brand creators with location flexibility often consider:

| Factor | Best |
|--------|------|
| Lowest corporate tax | Singapore (effective 8.5% on first SGD 200K) |
| Lowest personal tax | Singapore (caps at 24% for top bracket) |
| Easiest cross-border banking | Singapore (DBS, OCBC, UOB) |
| English-language legal system | Singapore, Philippines |
| Lowest cost of living | Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam |
| Best digital nomad visa | Thailand (LTR), Malaysia (DE Rantau) |
| Easiest to incorporate from abroad | Singapore (Pte Ltd remotely) |

**Common path:** Incorporate Pte Ltd in SG (low tax + global credibility), reside in TH/MY/ID/PH on long-term visa, manage clients globally.

## Anti-patterns (SEA personal brand tax)

1. **Operating below the radar in cash** -> can't prove income for visa renewal / bank loan / property
2. **Wrong entity for income level** (e.g. sole prop with USD 200K -> should be Pte Ltd / Sdn Bhd)
3. **No VAT/GST registration above threshold** -> back taxes + penalties
4. **Mixing personal and business banking** -> bookkeeping nightmare
5. **Not understanding tax residency** -> double taxation or accidental tax evasion across borders

## SEA-specific checklist

- [ ] Entity formed (country-appropriate sole prop or limited)
- [ ] Tax registration completed (national tax authority)
- [ ] VAT / GST / SST registration if above threshold
- [ ] Separate business bank account
- [ ] Bookkeeping software (Xero, QuickBooks, local equivalents)
- [ ] Annual tax filing scheduled
- [ ] If non-resident: tax residency clearly established
- [ ] If cross-border: tax treaty checked (e.g. SG-US, SG-DE, MY-AU, TH-UK)
- [ ] Payment processing set up (Stripe, Wise, etc.)
- [ ] Local accountant engaged (HIGHLY recommended given country complexity)
