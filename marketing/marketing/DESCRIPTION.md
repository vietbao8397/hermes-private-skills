# Fullstack Marketing Skills

A collection of 16 specific fullstack marketing skills, 4 AI marketing agents, and 3 workflows tailored for the Vietnam market framework by Over Powers Agency.

## 4 Marketing Agents
These agents' capabilities are inherently encoded inside the 16 skills provided here.
- **MKT Strategist**: Lập kế hoạch, nghiên cứu, định vị
- **Content Producer**: Sản xuất nội dung, script, copy quảng cáo
- **Performance Analyst**: Đọc data, đánh giá ads, báo cáo
- **Channel Operator**: Vận hành kênh, landing page, email

## 3 Workflows (Chuỗi làm việc)
Refer to the `_references` artifacts for the detailed workflow guides:
* `campaign-launch.md` (14-21 days strategy)
* `monthly-cycle.md` (3-5 days reporting)
* `content-production.md` (weekly routine)
